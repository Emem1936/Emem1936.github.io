As we give emphasis to our employee selection process in a unique way, and that “Character” of a person is the basic principle that matters above all. This methodology was attributed to harmony within our organization that widened our trades both internal and external operations.

Our expertise in the field of Human Resources has been attributed to our continued development of training programs in the areas of; Technical Skills, Information Technology, Onshore & Offshore Safety Management, Drilling, and Rig Move Crew. This Manpower Support Service we extend with various companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and within the Gulf Coordinating Council (GCC) including African countries.

International alliances, within the oil/gas industry, training and recruitment professionals from different countries; USA, Canada, United Kingdom (UK), Europe, Australia, India, Malaysia, and the Philippines has strengthened our foundation as a multi-cultural background entity.

Our people are the driving force and paramount above all the success behind our business endeavors.
