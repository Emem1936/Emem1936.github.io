THE COMPANY

AME Rig Movers “trade name”, a division of Adel Mohammed Ahmed Edris entity was established and organized by a well experienced and professional group of more than 20 years, coupled with extensive exposure in the oil and gas onshore rig move industry.

CAPABILITIES

Our competency which we provide comprehensive and detailed planning; route map, pre-move, safety, lifting plans, mobilization, and demobilization including milestone schedules to monitor the proper management during and after rig move operations.

QHSE ASSURANCE & MANPOWER RESOURCES

Paramount to our rig move services is all about the QHSE and HR management, and the qualifications of our key personnel manning the operations.They are full-pledged, certified and veteran rig movers headed by our Rig Move Manager, Operation Manager, Logistic Manager, Senior Truck Pusher, Junior Truck Pusher, Medic/Safety Officer, Rigger-I, including our truck drivers and heavy equipment operators are the forefront of our company’s foundation. A team of multi-task and well-versed in handling various types of workover and drilling rigs movement ranging capacities; 750HP, 1000HP, 1500HP, 2000HP & 3000HP.

COMMUNICATIONS

A vital aspect of our operations, both internal and external remote areas are critically important and essential part of our rig move for tracking the entire rig movement activities using the latest technology through the internet and satellite telecommunication systems including GPS monitoring apparatus.

FACILITIES, TRUCKS & HEAVY EQUIPMENT

Our logistic facilities are well-organized located in the Dammam area, and these facilities consist of a site office, accommodation, maintenance yard, warehouse, in-house training/orientation room, and recreation areas. This includes; Titan, Kenworth 953, Kenworth C500, Trailer- Lowbed, Trailer-Flatbed, Crane (Rough Terrain), Wheel Loader-Forklift, Dolly, Tanker-Diesel/Water, Manlift equipment, and support vehicles are the company’s premier assets.
